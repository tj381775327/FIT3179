Hospital Beds Used Visualisations

This scatter plot shows the correlation between population and bed use. We randomly selected six states with a relatively high total number of people with coronavirus infections as our display. If you want to see more, you can click the website..

The map show the distribution on hospital bed of USA.The color of the map is classified according to the number of beds used.
